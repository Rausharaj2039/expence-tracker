Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Spendly handles your information to provide a secure and efficient expense tracking experience.

1. Data We Collect

To provide the service, we collect basic information including:

  • Account details (email and password) for authentication.
  • Expense records (amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions) that you manually enter.
  • User preferences and settings.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Maintain your personal account and secure login.
  • Calculate spending summaries and generate financial insights.
  • Allow you to manage and delete your own data.

3. Data Storage

Your financial data is stored securely in our database. We employ industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. You have full control over your data and can delete your records at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or rent your personal financial data to third parties. We do not use external trackers or analytics services that compromise your privacy.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the way we handle your data, please contact us at support@spendly.example.
